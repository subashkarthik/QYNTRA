# LEXIMERA Brand Design Prompts

## Logo Generation Prompt
**Subject:** Minimalist application icon for "LEXIMERA"
**Concept:** Abstract letter "L" formed by connected neural nodes and data lines, or a minimal bracket shape.
**Style:** Flat, vector-like, glassmorphism, tech-native.
**Colors:** Deep Indigo (#4f46e5) and Electric Cyan (#06b6d4).
**Background:** Dark slate / Midnight blue (#020617).
**Details:**
- No text.
- High contrast.
- Geometric precision.
- Glowing nodes.
- 1:1 Aspect ratio.

**Negative Prompt:** Complex details, 3D render, drop shadows, mascot, face, robot, messy lines, low contrast.
